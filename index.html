Thanks for your interest in Soundkiss Studio, Taipei Taiwan ROC!

This is a legacy page for a recording studio that I established in 2011, which I closed the door to for the final time in 2015.

The same day, I was rushed to the airport for a flight to London. Only when the plane was taking off did it fully sink in that I was leaving my home and life of 8 years, and everyone and everything that was part of it.

Years on and many more gigs, recordings and tours under my belt, I still think about that other life, and the studio sessions, friendships and the records we made over four years.

This page is my dedication to it.
