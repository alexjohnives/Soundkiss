Foreword

This is a legacy web page for Soundkiss, a recording studio I established in 2011 in Taipei Taiwan and closed in 2015.

The day I closed the studio door for the last time marked the end of 8 years living in Taiwan as I had to rush to the airport to catch a flight to London. Only when the plane was taking off did I fully realise everything I was leaving behind.

Years on and many more gigs, recordings and tours under my belt, I still remember that other life; the studio sessions, friendships and the records we made.

This page is my record of it.

Background

Having had years playing in bands then working in live sound in the UK, when I travelled to Taiwan in 2008 to pursue Mandarin studies, I was immediately drawn to Taipei’s music scene, especially the many noisy sweaty underground clubs.


Alex

As I started playing out in bands and working sound I became known to local artists and was increasingly asked if I would record.

At the time I was also thinking of something new so I came up with a concept and decided to open my own studio.

線世瑋

From the outset, I shared my ideas with my then partner Crystal Shien, who offered much belief and support.

She agreed to join me as my Taiwanese partner, and became integral to making the studio and sessions work.
