Welcome and thankyou for your interest in Soundkiss

Coloured Whale in session at Soundkiss 2014

This is a legacy web page for Soundkiss, a recording studio I established and ran in Taipei, Taiwan ROC from 2011-2015.

The same day we closed I had to rush to the airport to catch a flight home to London, and so ended 8 years of live in Taiwan. Only when the plane was taking off did I fully realise everything I was leaving behind.

Years on and many more gigs, recordings and tours under my belt, I still remember that other life. The four years running Soundkiss were some of the best with the sessions, friendships and records we made.

This page is my record.


Background

As I started playing out in bands and working sound I became known to local artists and was increasingly asked if I would record.

I was also thinking it was time for something new, and the impossible dream of running a studio suddenly didn't seem so far fetched. I came up with a concept and decided to go for it.

From the outset, I shared my ideas with my then partner Crystal Shien, who as a musician herself saw the potential for making something happen.

She agreed to join me as my Taiwanese business partner, and would became integral to making the studio and sessions work.

Most important from the beginning was finding a large enough premises, in central Taipei with enough going for it.
